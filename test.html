Upcoming Gigs

ROOTS, ROCK, REGGAE
Are you wondering how these three things go together? Well…

The Rock element will be brought by amazing Bathurst born band “Liquid Culture”, supporting the night blending original rock with powerful covers, including Led Zeppelin classics.

For the Roots & Reggae, we’re bringing our beloved local band Javier & The Wave! We don’t need to say much about these guys but this:
Their career is spreading beyond Orange, making this show the first and last local one of the year where you’ll be able to see the full 6 piece band magic in Orange.

Liquid Culture blends original rock with powerful covers, including Led Zeppelin classics. After years of backyard jamming, the band has made great achievement: a slot at the 2024 Under Western Skies Festival, a regional tour with Music NSW and support for Camino Gold during Great Southern Nights. LC will bring the high-energy and fresh sounds of Rock.

Javier & The Wave is a high-vibe fusion of Reggae, Latin, and R&B, led by Chilean frontman Javier Rocha. Born from a love of nature, culture, and hopes for humanity, their music carries messages of freedom, unity, and joy, resonating from Australia to South America, New Zealand, and beyond. Known for their powerful original tracks and sold-out Bob Marley tribute shows, the band creates an unforgettable experience that leaves audiences feeling inspired and uplifted.

The food will be provided by the Orange Pie Company and we have 14 beers and ciders on tap as well as spritz drinks and a great local wine list.

PLEASE NOTE: There is no allocated seating for our ticketed events. Taproom open from 4pm so get here early to secure your preferred spot. Band on stage around 7:30pm
